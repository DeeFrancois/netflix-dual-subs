Turning ON Dual Subtitles:

Step 1: Right click => "Translate to.."

Step 2: Pin the extension button on the top right for easy access to customization options

Turning OFF Dual Subtitles:

Step 1: Open the customization box and click the off switch

Step 2: Use the Google Translate icon at the end of the address bar to deactivate the browser's translator
