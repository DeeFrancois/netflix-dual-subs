Turning ON Dual Subtitles:

1) Turn on subtitles in your Target Language

2) Activate the browser's translator with: Right Click => "Translate to.."

Turning OFF Dual Subtitles:

1) Uncheck "Enable Dual Subtitles" in Preferences

2) Use the Google Translate icon at the end of the address bar to deactivate the browser's translator before unchecking the box
